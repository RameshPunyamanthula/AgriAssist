Powdery Mildew Detected

Confidence: 90%

Powdery mildew is a fungal disease causing white, powdery spots on leaves and stems, hindering photosynthesis and reducing yield. Early intervention is key.

Crop Health Management & Prevention

Immediate Treatment:

  • Apply appropriate fungicides as per recommended dosage.
  • Remove and destroy severely infected plant parts.
  • Improve air circulation around plants.

Long-term Prevention:

  • Plant disease-resistant crop varieties.
  • Practice crop rotation to break disease cycles.
  • Ensure proper spacing and irrigation to reduce humidity.
  • Maintain optimal soil health and nutrient balance.

For severe or persistent crop health issues, consult an agricultural extension officer or plant pathologist.

Aphid Infestation Detected

Confidence: 88%

Aphids are small sap-sucking insects that can cause stunted growth, distorted leaves, and transmit plant viruses. Early control is vital to prevent crop damage.

Pest/Weed Management

Control Measures:

  • Apply appropriate insecticides or organic pest control methods.
  • Implement manual weeding or use herbicides for severe weed infestations.
  • Introduce beneficial insects (e.g., ladybugs for aphids).

Preventive Strategies:

  • Practice regular field scouting to detect issues early.
  • Use certified, pest/weed-free seeds.
  • Employ crop rotation and proper sanitation.

Implement integrated pest management (IPM) strategies and consult local agricultural experts for persistent issues.
